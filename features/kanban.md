The Kanban view shows your account plans as cards in columns. Each column represents a plan status. You can drag and drop cards to change status quickly.

---

## Opening Kanban View

1. Go to **Account Planning**.
2. Click **Kanban View**.

---

## Understanding the Columns

| Column | Status |
|--------|--------|
| **Draft** | Plans not yet started |
| **In Progress** | Active plans being worked on |
| **Review** | Plans under review |
| **Completed** | Finished plans |
| **Archived** | Archived plans |

---

## How to Use Kanban

### Viewing Plans

- Each card shows key info (subject, client, maybe date or status).
- Scroll within a column to see more cards.
- Scroll horizontally to see all columns.

### Changing Status (Drag and Drop)

1. Click and hold a card.
2. Drag it to another column.
3. Release.
4. The plan's status will update automatically.

**Note:** You need **Edit** permission to drag and drop.
