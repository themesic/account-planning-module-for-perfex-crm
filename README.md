# Account Planning Module for Perfex CRM

Welcome to the **Account Planning Module** documentation. This guide will help you understand, install, and use the module to manage strategic account planning within your Perfex CRM.

---

## What is Account Planning?

**Account Planning** is a customer-centric approach to managing your most important clients. It helps you:

- **Identify priority accounts** – Know which clients deserve the most attention
- **Capture critical information** – Store vision, mission, financial data, and marketing activities in one place
- **Analyze opportunities and threats** – Use the BCG model and client status to assess each account
- **Develop growth strategies** – Create objectives, tasks, and action plans to expand relationships
- **Track progress** – Link plans to projects, invoices, and estimates for a complete picture

## Quick Start

1. **Install** the module in Perfex CRM (Setup → Modules → Upload).
2. **Activate** it and enter your purchase code.
3. **Assign permissions** to staff who will use it.
4. **Create your first account plan** from the Account Planning menu.

For detailed steps, go to [Installation](getting-started/installation.md).

---

## Requirements

- **Perfex CRM** version 2.3 or higher
- A valid purchase license from CodeCanyon
- Staff with appropriate permissions (view, create, edit, delete)
