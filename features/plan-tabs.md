# Plan Tabs and Sections

When you open an account plan, you see several tabs on the left. Each tab holds different types of information. This page explains each one.

---

## Tab Overview

| Tab | Icon | Purpose |
|-----|------|---------|
| Due Diligence | User | Financial data, marketing activities |
| Team Information | Users | Your team and client team |
| Service Ability Offering | Sticky note | Services and offerings |
| Planning | Chart | Objectives, tasks, MindMap, status |
| Relations | Link | Projects, invoices, estimates |
| Meeting Notes | Note | Call logs and meeting notes |
| Competitors | Competitor | Competitor tracking |
| Activity | Log | Activity history |

---

## Due Diligence

**What it contains:**

- **Financial data** – Year, revenue, sales spent, traffic, loss (spreadsheet table)
- **Marketing activities** – Item and reference (spreadsheet table)

**How to use:**

1. Click the **Due Diligence** tab.
2. Edit the tables directly (add rows, type in cells).
3. Click **Submit** to save.

---

## Team Information

**What it contains:**

- **Your company team** – Staff assigned to this account
- **Client team** – Client contacts and their details (name, title, email, phone, etc.)

**How to use:**

1. Click **Team Information**.
2. Add or edit team members.
3. Save your changes.

---

## Service Ability Offering

**What it contains:**

- **Current Service** – Services the client currently uses
- **Service Ability Offering** – Your offerings with potential, scale, prioritization

**How to use:**

1. Click **Service Ability Offering**.
2. Edit the spreadsheet tables.
3. Click **Submit** to save.

---

## Planning

This is the main strategic tab. It includes:

### Basic Info

- **Subject** – Plan title
- **Time** – Plan period
- **Plan Status** – Draft, In Progress, Review, Completed, Archived
- **Client Status** – Red, Yellow, Green (traffic-light style)
- **BCG Model** – Question marks, Stars, Dogs, Cash cows

### Objectives and Strategy

- **Objectives** – Main goals (rich text editor)
- **Revenue next year** – Planned revenue
- **Margin** – Margin percentage
- **Wallet share** – Wallet share percentage
- **Threat** – Threats (rich text)
- **Opportunity** – Opportunities (rich text)
- **Criteria to success** – Success criteria
- **Constraints** – Constraints

### TODO List (Tasks)

- **Objectives** – Group tasks by objective
- **Items** – Sub-items under each objective
- **Tasks** – Action needed, assignee (PIC), deadline, status, priority

**Task actions:**

- **Convert to Task** – Create a Perfex task from a plan task (status syncs both ways)
- **Edit** – Change task details
- **Delete** – Remove the task

### MindMap

- A **MindMap** editor for visual strategy planning.
- Use the **LAMP icon** to create or edit the MindMap.
- Export as PNG or SVG from the Planning tab.

### Goals & KPIs

- Add goals with target, actual, and due date.
- Track progress toward key metrics.

### Plan Health Score

- A calculated health score (percentage) based on tasks and progress.
- Shown at the top of the Planning tab when available.

### Approval

- **Approved by** – Shows who approved the plan and when (if used).

---

## Relations

**What it contains:**

- **Linked items** – Projects, Invoices, Estimates, Proposals
- **Quick Create Project** – Create a new project linked to this plan
- **Linked project tasks & milestones** – Tasks and milestones from linked projects

**How to use:**

1. Click **Add Relation** and choose Project, Invoice, Estimate, or Proposal.
2. Select the item from the dropdown and save.
3. Use **Quick Create Project** to create a new project and link it automatically.
4. Linked project tasks and milestones appear below the relations table.

See [Relations](relations.md) for more details.

---

## Meeting Notes

**What it contains:**

- **Meeting notes / call logs** – Date and notes for each meeting or call

**How to use:**

1. Click **Add meeting note**.
2. Enter the date and notes.
3. Save.
4. Use **Edit** or **Delete** to modify existing notes.

---

## Competitors

**What it contains:**

- **Competitor name** – Name of the competitor
- **Threat level** – Low, Medium, or High
- **Notes** – Additional notes

**How to use:**

1. Click **Add competitor**.
2. Enter name, threat level, and notes.
3. Save.
4. Use **Edit** or **Delete** to modify existing entries.

---

## Activity

**What it contains:**

- **Activity log** – History of changes (created, updated, status changes, etc.)

This tab is read-only. It helps you see who did what and when.

---

## Plan Actions (Dropdown)

When viewing a plan, use the **dropdown menu** (caret) next to the plan title for:

- **Export PDF** – Download the plan as PDF
- **Create next period** – Copy this plan to the next month/quarter
- **Compare plans** – Compare two plans side by side
- **Delete** – Delete the plan (requires permission)

---

## Client Update Requests

If a client has requested an update via the client portal, you will see an alert at the top with a link to **Mark as handled**. Use this to acknowledge and close the request.
