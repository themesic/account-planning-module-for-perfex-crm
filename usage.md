# How to Use Account Planning Module

The use of the module may vary depending on different conditions and the scope of every webmaster or staff member. Different companies have different needs and different approaches to their clients—all of which can be applied through this account planning tool.

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to **ACCOUNT PLANNING > ADD NEW** in your Perfex CRM admin menu
2. Create your first account planning project
3. Explore the module's features and capabilities

## Key Workflows

Each account planning project can be customized with:

- **Due Diligence** – Conduct and document client due diligence
- **Team Information** – Manage team details and assignments
- **Service Ability Offering** – Define and track service offerings
- **Project Planning** – Plan and track project phases
- **Strategy MindMaps** – Visualize strategy and relationships

Feel free to customize the sections and workflows to match your organization's needs.
