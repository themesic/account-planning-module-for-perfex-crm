# Installation

This guide walks you through installing the Account Planning module in Perfex CRM. No technical experience is required—just follow the steps in order.

---

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

- **Perfex CRM** installed and working (version 2.3 or higher)
- **Admin access** to your Perfex CRM
- The **module ZIP file** from your CodeCanyon purchase
- Your **purchase code** (you will need it for activation)

---

## Step 1: Download the Module

1. Log in to your **CodeCanyon** account.
2. Go to **Downloads** and find the **Account Planning Module for Perfex CRM**.
3. Download the **ZIP file** (do not unzip it).

---

## Step 2: Upload the Module in Perfex

1. Log in to your **Perfex CRM** as an administrator.
2. Go to **Setup** (gear icon) → **Modules**.
3. Find the **Upload Module** section.
4. Click **Choose File** and select the downloaded ZIP file.
5. Click **Upload**.

![Upload Module](https://via.placeholder.com/600x200?text=Upload+Module+Section)

---

## Step 3: Verify the Upload

After the upload completes:

- The module should appear in the **Modules** list.
- Its status will show as **Inactive** until you activate it.
- The module name will be **Account Planning**.

If you see an error message, check that:

- The ZIP file was not corrupted during download
- Your Perfex CRM version is 2.3 or higher
- You have write permissions on the server

---

## Step 4: Activate the Module

Before you can use the module, you must activate it with your purchase code. See [Activation](activation.md) for detailed steps.

---

## What Happens During Installation?

When you upload and activate the module, it will:

- Create the necessary database tables for account plans
- Add the **Account Planning** menu item to the sidebar
- Create default settings (e.g., reminder days, client portal)
- Register permissions for staff
