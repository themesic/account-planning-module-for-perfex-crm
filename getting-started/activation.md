# Activation

After installing the Account Planning module, you must activate it with your purchase code. This step verifies your license and enables all features.

---

## Where to Find Your Purchase Code

1. Log in to **CodeCanyon**.
2. Go to **Downloads**.
3. Find **Account Planning Module for Perfex CRM**.
4. Click **Download** and choose **License certificate & purchase code**.
5. Your **Purchase Code** will be shown (it looks like: `xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx`).

---

## How to Activate

1. In Perfex CRM, go to **Setup** → **Modules**.
2. Find **Account Planning** in the list.
3. Click **Activate** (or the activation link).
4. Enter your **Purchase Code** when prompted.
5. Click **Activate** or **Verify**.

---

## Activation Successful

When activation succeeds:

- The module status changes to **Active**.
- The **Account Planning** menu appears in the sidebar.
- All features become available.

---

## Activation Failed

If activation fails, you may see a message such as:

> "One of your modules failed its verification and got deactivated."

**Common causes:**

| Cause | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Wrong purchase code | Copy the code again from CodeCanyon; avoid extra spaces. |
| Code already used elsewhere | Each license is typically for one site. Use the correct license for this installation. |
| Server cannot reach Envato | Check firewall or server restrictions. |
| Invalid or expired license | Contact the module author or CodeCanyon support. |

---

## Reactivation

If you move your site or reinstall Perfex:

1. Go to **Setup** → **Modules**.
2. Find **Account Planning**.
3. Use the activation option again with your purchase code.
