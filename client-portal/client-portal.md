## Enabling the Client Portal

1. Go to **Account Planning** ‚Üí **Settings**.
2. Check **Enable client portal view for account plans**.
3. Click **Save**.

---

## What Clients See

### Account Planning Menu

- Clients see **Account Planning** in their client portal sidebar.
- Clicking it opens a list of their account plans.

### Plans List

The list shows:

- **ID** ‚Äì Plan number
- **Subject** ‚Äì Plan title
- **Plan Status** ‚Äì Draft, In Progress, Review, Completed, Archived
- **Date** ‚Äì Plan period
- **View** ‚Äì Button to open the plan

Only plans for **their** client account are visible.

---

## Viewing a Plan

When a client clicks **View** on a plan:

- They see the plan in **read-only** mode.
- They can browse tabs (Due Diligence, Team Information, Planning, etc.).
- They **cannot** edit anything.

---

## Request Update

Clients can request that staff update the plan:

1. While viewing a plan, they click **Request update** (or similar).
2. A request is sent to staff.
3. Staff see an alert on the plan: "Client requested plan update ‚Äì X pending request(s)."
4. Staff can **Mark as handled** when the update is done.

---

## Mobile-Friendly

The client portal views are designed to work on mobile devices:

- Tables use responsive layout.
- Buttons and links are touch-friendly.
- Content adapts to smaller screens.

---

## Permissions

- **Client portal access** is controlled by the **Enable client portal view** setting.
- **Staff permissions** (View, Create, Edit, Delete) do not affect what clients see.
- Clients only see plans for their own account.

---

## Disabling the Client Portal

1. Go to **Account Planning** ‚Üí **Settings**.
2. Uncheck **Enable client portal view for account plans**.
3. Save.

The Account Planning menu will no longer appear in the client portal.
